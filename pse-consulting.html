To our potential clients

I would like to thank you for your interest in working with our professional organization, Pi Sigma Epsilon. Pi Sigma Epsilon (PSE) is the premier co-ed marketing, sales, and management fraternity at UC Berkeley. We develop our members' professional skills and prepare them for the real world through workshops, info sessions, and team projects.
Every semester, we work on projects with several companies ranging from startups to corporations. We have completed marketing and consulting projects for companies involved in technology, retail, social media, dining, and philanthropy. We hope to work on projects that not only benefit our partnering companies, but to also teach our members real life skills they cannot obtain in a classroom.
PSE is available to suit any of your company's needs, whether it is learning more about your consumer marketing or capturing more consumers through marketing strategies. Our members are eager to apply the skills they have learned in class to real world scenarios that will affect the lives of those around them. Financial compensation for our work is based on a negotiable hourly rate or by a stipend for a semester worth of work. Compensation varies depending on the projects' needs and number of members required per project.

Best,
Catie Fan
Director of Projects
