About

Pi Sigma Epsilon strives to provide its brothers with invaluable opportunities for professional development. We host workshops, career panels, and networking events in addition to social events and corporate projects throughout the semester. Our dynamic brotherhood is small, diverse, and tight-knit - the relationships you build are strong and meaningful.



We value practical, hands-on experiences over busy work, and trust the leadership and potential of all of our members - even our newest ones.



As a new organization, we thrive in an exciting, fast-paced environment in which the opportunities for personal and professional growth are limitless.
