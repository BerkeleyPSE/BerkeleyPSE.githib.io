Our History

Pi Sigma Epsilon (PSE) is the only national, co-ed, professional fraternal organization in sales, marketing, and management. PSE offers practical sales and marketing experience to members through active involvement in sales and marketing projects, marketing research, professional programs, and social events.
The Zeta Chi chapter of PSE was started by Sabrina Ruiz in Feruary 2013. She observed a lack of marketing, sales, and retail management courses and organizations on campus despite the number of interested students and recruiters, and believed PSE could be the solution. To make this idea into a reality, she recruited Kelly Chao, Cynthia Huang, Eunbyul (Lydia) Kim, Pei (Peggy) Lin, and Hiu Ngan to promote the chapter's founding.
We have since grown from a small group of 7 to a chapter of over 40 dedicated individuals. Our members are diverse and highly motivated individuals who show interest in the advancement of marketing and sales management as a career and a profession.
